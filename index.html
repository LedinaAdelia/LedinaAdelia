6 June, 2022
Balanced nutrition plays an important role in the physical and psychological growth of children. Physical activity is any body movement that contributes to the oxidation of fat, for each energy expenditure based on the level of intensity of activity carried out. Good quality children can be achieved by ensuring that the child's growth process is also good. The digital health monitoring system provides an opportunity to increase the reach, involvement, and intensity of parents in improving the quality of nutrition and physical activity of children.

The information system was built with PHP and Mysql and the calculation of nutritional needs using the Harris-Benedict method. The Harris-Benedict method is a way of calculating the number of calories a person needs. The programming languages used for making this application are PHP and MySQL for the database. Based on the research results, this application can help parents to find out the nutritional needs of their children who are growing up online.
